# Exemplo: Retrato Corporativo

## Invocação

```
/gabs-design
```

## Informações fornecidas

- Objetivo: retrato editorial para LinkedIn
- Sujeito: homem, rosto redondo, sobrancelhas grossas, barba curta
- Ferramenta: Gemini / Imagen 3
- Estilo: fotorrealismo editorial de estúdio
- Formato: 4:5

## Output esperado

```
🎯 FERRAMENTA: Gemini / Imagen 3

📐 PROMPT PRINCIPAL:
Subject & Identity: Hyper-realistic portrait of a man, rounded face,
thick eyebrows, short light beard. Neutral, confident expression.
Arms relaxed.

Camera & Angle: Medium shot, 85mm lens, f/2.8 for shallow depth of field.

Lighting: High-end editorial studio. Soft diffused lighting (giant softbox),
gentle shadows under nose and chin. Light gray seamless background.

Wardrobe: Dark tailored blazer, plain white shirt. Matte fabric textures.

Technical Specs: 8k resolution, RAW photo style, visible pores,
subsurface scattering, catchlight in eyes, Phase One IQ4.

🚫 NEGATIVE PROMPT:
Plastic skin, doll-like, airbrushed, cartoon, 3D render, illustration,
distorted eyes, text, watermark, smiling, open mouth.
```
